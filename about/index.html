About Lucente QR

Lucente QR is a live digital QR-code creation tool developed under Lucente Corporate. It helps users generate and customise QR codes for practical personal and business uses.

Status: Live

What it helps users do

Users can create QR codes for website links, text, email, phone numbers, Wi-Fi details, and business contact cards, then customise colours and download the result.

Relationship to Lucente Corporate

Lucente QR is a project by Lucente Corporate, the independent technology brand founded by Sahaan Kesavan and Farris Zaman.
